# Outline - The Baker Who Outsmarted the CEO

## Act 1
- Ch1: Maya's world — Chen's Hearth, the neighborhood, her grandmother's legacy. Derek Holt makes a buyout offer.
- Ch2: Maya refuses. Derek escalates — health inspector citations, lease pressure, public dismissal.

## Act 2
- Ch3: Maya discovers Derek's new "artisan" product line is built on stolen local recipes, including hers.
- Ch4: Derek presents a licensing deal disguised as a lifeline. Maya nearly signs.

## Act 3
- Ch5: Rosa reveals Maya's grandmother secretly trademarked the sourdough formula. Derek's company has been infringing for months.
- Ch6: Derek plants a smear story. Priya investigates and finds the real story runs the other direction.

## Act 4
- Ch7: Confrontation at the city food expo — Maya arrives with proof as Derek prepares to publicly launch the stolen line.
- Ch8: The reversal — Derek's brand collapses under the exposure. The neighborhood rallies. Maya's bakery becomes the story everyone wants to tell.
