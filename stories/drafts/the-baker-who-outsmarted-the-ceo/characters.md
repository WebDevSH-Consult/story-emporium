# Characters - The Baker Who Outsmarted the CEO

## Protagonist
Name: Maya Chen
Role: Underdog baker, owner of Chen's Hearth
Traits: Self-taught, stubborn, deeply loyal to her grandmother's legacy, terrible at self-promotion but brilliant at flavor

## Antagonist
Name: Derek Holt
Role: CEO of Holt Foods, a food conglomerate masquerading as an artisan brand
Traits: Polished, manipulative, uses legal and political leverage to crush small competitors

## Supporting Characters
- Rosa Delgado — Maya's neighbor and grandmother's oldest friend; keeper of old secrets
- Jamie Park — Maya's 22-year-old assistant baker; loyal, observant, morally sharp
- Priya Nair — independent food journalist; doesn't trust Derek, follows the story wherever it leads
- Councilwoman Sandra Torres — local politician in Derek's pocket; later forced to reckon with her choices
