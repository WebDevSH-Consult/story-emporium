# Book Bible: AI to Income

## Core Information
- **Target Reader:** 40-50 year old Basic Computer user.
- **Tone & Voice:** Authoritative yet as a teacher. Encouraging, clear, and jargon-free.
- **Purpose:** Passive income. Empowering non-technical users to build online assets.
- **Promise:** Transform complete beginners into digital builders who can create and monetize full-stack websites using AI.

## Reader Journey
- **Current State:** Frustrated by perceived complexity of tech, feeling left behind.
- **Aha! Moment:** AI makes coding a matter of clear communication, not memorizing syntax.
- **End State:** Digital asset owner with the skills to build and earn independently.
